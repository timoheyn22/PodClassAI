From slides to sound - work smarter, not harder

PodClass AI

Load up your lectures, convert them into a podcast and listen to them on the go!

How does it work?

Just convert your lecture slides or your notes into a PDF and load them them up on this website. You can then choose specifics as language or length of podcast. With the help of AI, your notes will be converted into a podcast you can listen to whenever you want, wherever you want.

What is it good for?

“Learning by listening” is one of the six types of learning. Some people process information better by listening, instead of reading your lecture again and again. Transforming them into a podcast rather than just letting them be read out loud by a program helps you to engage with them more and create an easy listening experience.
